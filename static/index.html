Once you have the new wallpaper image on your device, open your Wallpaper settings and select the image.

Make sure it's set to "Still", zoomed out all the way, and dragged to the bottom of the screen. Don't worry if it looks like there's too much black space at the top of the screen; it'll fit just right once you save it.
